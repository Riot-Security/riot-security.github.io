Passive Recon

Passive Recon is our most hands-off service, no scans or tools will actively be used on the service/network, this requires gathering intel from public sources typically found on the web, such as investigating a website, anaylyzing a code, viewing registered domains&subdomains, etc.
This service does not include a report, only potential (unverified) vunerabilities.
Choose this service if you're not sure if you should get a full pentest.

Web Application Pentest

Webapplication testing, means we'll investigate your website or website related services for vunerabilities. This service may use: scanners, tools, scripts, proxies, and attack methods such as injection, brute forcing, or permission abuse. Typically a full report is offered with this service.
